Full course syllabus

6 modules - from taxation basics to compliance, filing, and accounting links

Module 1 Introduction to Taxation
  • Taxation: how it works in Nepal
  • Types of taxes and tax authorities
  • Income year and assessment year concept
  • Basic tax rules and glossary
Module 2 PAN and VAT Registration
  • PAN registration for individuals and businesses
  • VAT registration for business
  • Criteria for VAT registration
  • IRD taxpayer portal basics
Module 3 Income Tax for Business
  • Business income and taxable income
  • Allowable expenses, deductions and exemptions
  • Set-off and carry forward of business losses
  • Basic annual tax liability calculation
Module 4 Tax Rates and Tax Payment
  • Tax slab concept for different taxpayers
  • Tax for sole proprietorship, firm and company
  • Estimated tax and installment payment
  • Tax on rent, contract, interest, land and securities
Module 5 TDS, VAT and Compliance
  • TDS concept and calculation
  • TDS on salary, rent, service, contract and interest
  • Employee tax deduction and adjustment
  • e-TDS return filing and final withholding tax
  • VAT basics and billing system
  • Taxable, zero-rated and VAT-exempt items
  • Input VAT, output VAT and VAT payable
  • Monthly VAT return filing and payment
Module 6 Records, Filing and Accounting Link
  • Purchase book and sales book preparation
  • VAT credit management and adjustment
  • VAT reconciliation and Annex 13 preparation
  • VAT inclusive price and compliance process
  • Income tax return filing process
  • Tax clearance certificate, refund and recovery
  • Arrears, adjustment, fines and penalties
  • Real business cases and practical assignment
  • Purchase, sales and expense entry
  • Cash, bank and contra transaction
  • Fixed assets and depreciation concept
  • Tax invoice record and business reports
