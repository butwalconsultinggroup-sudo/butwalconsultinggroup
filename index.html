FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about our consulting services and accounting training programs.

What services does Butwal Consulting Group provide?
Butwal Consulting Group provides end-to-end business support including accounting system implementation, tax and compliance services, loan projection, financial planning, and study abroad application support. Our goal is to make business, finance, and documentation work easier, more systematic, and professionally managed.
Do you help businesses set up accounting systems?
Yes. We help businesses design and implement accounting systems that make daily transactions, reporting, billing, and financial records easier to manage. Our support is practical and suitable for small businesses, suppliers, restaurants, offices, and growing companies.
Do you provide tax and compliance support?
Yes. We support clients with tax-related work such as monthly VAT return preparation, TDS management, compliance monitoring, and basic regulatory guidance. We focus on helping businesses keep their records clear, timely, and organized.
Can you help with loan projection and financial planning?
Yes. We prepare loan-ready financial projections, feasibility reports, and planning documents for business funding or loan application purposes. This helps clients present their financial position and future plan in a more professional way.
Do you provide study abroad application support?
Yes. We assist students with important documentation such as SOP, LOR, and application-related papers. Our support is focused on improving clarity, structure, and presentation so that students can submit stronger and better-organized documents.
What training programs do you offer?
We offer practical accounting and taxation-related training programs such as Basic Accounting, Advanced Taxation, Financial Reporting, and Cashier Training. These programs are designed for students, job seekers, employees, and business owners who want real-world skills.
